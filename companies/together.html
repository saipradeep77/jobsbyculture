Culture Overview

What it's really like to work at Together AI

Founded in 2022 by Vipul Ved Prakash, Together AI is an open-source AI infrastructure company building training and inference platforms. The culture is deeply engineering-driven with a flat hierarchy where everyone wears multiple hats. Expect a collaborative, low-ego environment where open-source contributions are celebrated and research output punches well above the team size.

The good and the hard parts

✓ What employees love

  • ✓ Open-source AI infrastructure — real mission alignment, not just marketing
  • ✓ Small team with outsized research output; your work has direct impact
  • ✓ Strong compensation for stage — competitive with much larger companies
  • ✓ Collaborative, low-ego culture where ideas win on merit

✗ What could be better

  • ✗ Early-stage means wearing many hats constantly — not for specialists
  • ✗ Limited career ladder; growth paths are still being defined
  • ✗ Fast-changing priorities as the AI infra landscape shifts rapidly
  • ✗ Less brand recognition than competitors like OpenAI or Anthropic
