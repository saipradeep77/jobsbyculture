Culture Overview

What it's like to work at Mistral AI

Mistral AI is Europe's leading AI lab, building open-weight foundation models that compete with the best in the world. Founded by ex-DeepMind and Meta researchers, the culture is intensely engineering-driven with a flat structure, fast shipping cadence, and a strong open-source philosophy. At ~100 people, everyone wears many hats.

How engineers build at Mistral AI

Mistral's engineering team builds open-weight models like Mixtral and Mistral Large, along with La Plateforme API for enterprise deployment. The stack is deeply technical with Python, C++, and CUDA at the core, reflecting the team's research-first approach to building frontier AI systems.
