Culture Overview

What it's like to work at Cohere

Cohere is building enterprise AI tools powered by large language models, founded by Aidan Gomez, a co-author of the original Transformer paper. The culture emphasizes learning and research, with an engineering-driven approach where team members wear many hats in a fast-moving startup environment.

Engineering Culture

How engineers build at Cohere

Cohere's engineering team works across the full research-to-product pipeline, building enterprise LLM APIs including Command, Embed, and Rerank. The stack centers on Python and Go, with a focus on turning cutting-edge research into production-grade APIs for enterprise customers.
