# JobsByCulture Monetization Playbook

## The Opportunity

Culture-first SMBs (50-300 employees) are underserved by existing job boards. Enterprise platforms like The Muse ($20k+/yr) and Glassdoor ($7k+/yr) are too expensive. Free platforms like Wellfound are too generic. **Nobody owns the "verified culture profile + niche job board" space for SMBs at an affordable price point.**

Key Values (keyvalues.com) tried this but never scaled beyond a one-person manual operation.

### Recommended JobsByCulture Pricing

| Tier | Price | What's Included |
|------|-------|----------------|
| **Free** | $0 | Basic culture profile (auto-generated from public data), 2 featured job posts, "Unclaimed" badge |
| **Verified** | $99/mo ($990/yr) | Claimed & customized profile, verified badge, 5 job posts, basic analytics (views/clicks), priority in search |
| **Growth** | $249/mo ($2,490/yr) | Everything in Verified + unlimited job posts, featured in blog posts & comparisons, advanced analytics, compare page prominence, "Culture-First Employer" badge |
| **Per-Post** | $199/post (30 days) | For companies that don't want a subscription — single job post with culture profile linkback |

**Why this pricing works:**
- **Free tier** builds the catalog and creates the "unclaimed" hook for outreach
- **$99/mo** is an easy yes for any Head of People with a recruiting budget (less than 1 LinkedIn job post)
- **$249/mo** is still 80-90% cheaper than Built In, Glassdoor, or The Muse
- **Annual pricing** gives you predictable revenue and lower churn

**Revenue targets:**
- 10 Verified customers = $990/mo (~$12k/yr)
- 20 Verified + 5 Growth = $3,225/mo (~$39k/yr)
- 50 Verified + 15 Growth = $8,685/mo (~$104k/yr)

## Daily Outreach Process

### Tools Needed (Total: $0 to start)

| Tool | Purpose | Cost |
|------|---------|------|
| Apollo.io (free tier) | Find contacts + emails + sequences | $0 (10K credits/mo) |
| Hunter.io (free tier) | Verify emails, find patterns | $0 (25 searches/mo) |
| LinkedIn (free) | Research contacts | $0 |
| LinkedIn Sales Nav (trial) | Build initial target list | $0 for 30 days |

### Daily Workflow (30-45 min/day)

**Monday:**
1. Pick 5 companies from the target list
2. Find Head of People / CEO on LinkedIn
3. Find their email via Apollo/Hunter
4. Send Template A or B (personalized with their actual profile link)
5. Log in spreadsheet: Company, Contact, Email, Date Sent

**Wednesday:**
1. Send Template C follow-ups to anyone from 5+ days ago with no reply
2. Pick 3 new companies, repeat Monday's process

**Friday:**
1. Send breakup emails (Template D) for 14+ day old non-responders
2. Review any replies, schedule calls
3. Update spreadsheet with outcomes

**Target:** 8-10 new outreach emails per week = 35-40/month

### Expected Conversion Funnel

| Stage | Rate | Monthly Volume |
|-------|------|---------------|
| Emails sent | — | 40 |
| Opens | 50% | 20 |
| Replies | 8-12% (above average because of profile hook) | 3-5 |
| Calls/claims | 50% of replies | 2-3 |
| Free → Verified conversion | 30% over 3 months | 1/month |

**Month 1-3:** Focus on getting 15-20 free claims. Build social proof.
**Month 4-6:** Convert free → Verified. Target 5-10 paying customers.
**Month 7-12:** Scale outreach, add Growth tier upsells. Target 20-30 paying.

## What to Build (Product Side)

Before outreach, you need:

1. **Claim flow** — Simple form where companies verify ownership (email domain match) and customize their profile (edit description, add benefits, upload logo, highlight values)
2. **"Verified" badge** — Visual indicator on the profile that the company has claimed and customized their page
3. **Job posting dashboard** — Simple way for verified companies to post/manage jobs
4. **Basic analytics** — Page views, click-throughs on "Apply" button (even a simple counter)
5. **Landing page** — `/for-employers` explaining the free tier and value prop

You do NOT need to build all of this before starting outreach. Start with manual claims (they email you, you customize their profile) and build the self-serve tools after you have 5-10 customers.

## Key Insight

**Your unfair advantage: you already did the work.** Every other job board sends cold emails saying "we can help you." You send emails saying "we already profiled your company and candidates are already seeing it." That's fundamentally different. The profile exists whether they claim it or not — which creates natural urgency without being pushy.
